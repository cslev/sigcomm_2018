## {{ page.title }}
The SIGCOMM 2018 Banquet will be held on Európa Ship from 7 pm and 11 pm on
Wednesday, August 22. Európa Ship will depart from Pier 5 at Vigadó Square
at 7:00 pm. The banquet will include an announcement of SIGCOMM 2019 and
Outrageous-Opinion Session.
